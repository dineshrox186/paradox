Knowledge Base

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I connect to the server?+

Open Minecraft Java Edition, go to Multiplayer > Add Server, and enter: play.paradoxsmp.net. We support 1.20+.

What version does the server run?+

Paradox SMP runs the latest stable Minecraft Java Edition (1.21). Bedrock is not supported.

I was unfairly banned. What do I do?+

Submit a ban appeal in our Discord #ban-appeals channel. Include your username and reason you believe the ban was incorrect.

How do I report a cheater or griefer?+

Use /report <username> in-game or post in #player-reports on Discord with evidence.

Do purchased ranks transfer across seasons?+

Yes! All ranks are permanent and carry over between seasons.

Can I get a refund on a purchase?+

All sales are final. Contact us for technical issues with a purchase.

How do I create a clan?+

Use /clan create <name> in-game. It costs $5,000 in-game currency. Invite with /clan invite <player>.
