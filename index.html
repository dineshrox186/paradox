FAQ
How do I connect to the server?+

Open Minecraft Java Edition, go to Multiplayer → Add Server, and enter play.paradoxsmp.net. We support 1.20 and above.

What Minecraft version does the server run?+

Paradox SMP runs the latest stable Minecraft Java Edition (1.21). Bedrock Edition is not supported at this time.

I was banned unfairly. What do I do?+

Submit a ban appeal in the Discord #ban-appeals channel. Include your username and a clear explanation of why you believe the ban was incorrect.

How do I report a cheater or griefer?+

Use /report <username> in-game, or post in #player-reports on Discord with screenshot or video evidence.

Do ranks carry over between seasons?+

Yes — all purchased ranks are permanent and transfer automatically to every new season.

Can I get a refund?+

All sales are final per our store policy. If you experienced a technical issue with your purchase, contact staff directly on Discord.

How do I create a clan?+

Use /clan create <name> in-game. Creating a clan costs $5,000 in-game currency. Invite members with /clan invite <player>.
